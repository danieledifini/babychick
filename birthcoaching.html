Birth Coaching: Virtual Birth Preparation Package

Preparing for the birth of your baby is an exciting and transformative journey. The Virtual Birth Preparation Package is designed to provide personalized, comprehensive guidance to help you navigate labor and delivery with confidence and ease. As a seasoned birth doula with 14 years of experience and over 300 births attended, Nina offers tailored support to meet your unique needs and preferences.

Whether you’re planning a hospital birth, birthing center delivery, or home birth, and whether you desire an unmedicated birth, medicated delivery, or cesarean birth, Nina’s extensive expertise ensures you feel informed and empowered. She’s here to help you achieve the birth experience you envision!

What's Included:

  • Two 2-Hour Prenatal Visits:Assess your goals for birth, explore options, and provide practical tools to help you feel prepared. Together, you’ll also create a postpartum plan to support you after your baby’s arrival.
  • Birth Preferences Assistance:Nina will help clarify your birth preferences and explain the various options available, ensuring your plan reflects your values and desires.
  • Continuous Virtual Support:During labor and birth, Nina offers emotional and informational virtual support to help you stay aligned with your vision (8 AM-8 PM CT, any day of the week).
  • Partner Support:Practical tips and comfort measures for your partner to confidently support you during labor.
  • Birth Plan Creation:Guidance to craft your perfect personalized, actionable birth plan.
  • Hospital Bag Packing Support:Answers to your questions about what to bring for your labor and delivery.
  • Ongoing Communication:Email and text support for general questions throughout the remainder of your pregnancy (Mon-Fri, 8 AM-5 PM CT).
  • Postpartum Session:An optional 30-minute virtual session to celebrate your baby’s birth, reflect on your birth experie

This package provides unparalleled virtual support and preparation for your labor and delivery, helping you and your partner feel ready and confident every step of the way. While it does not include in-person labor support, Nina’s extensive experience ensures you have the tools and knowledge to achieve a positive and empowering birth experience.
