Nursery Organization

Setting up your baby’s nursery can feel overwhelming, but with Nina’s help, you’ll have a beautifully organized space that’s ready for your little one’s arrival.

Whether you need hands-on assistance in person (limited availability) or virtual support, Nina will transform your nursery into a calm, functional haven for both you and your baby.

What's Included:

  • Virtual or In-Person Consultation: Nina will meet with you to discuss your nursery theme, room layout, needs, and must-have items to help create the ideal space for your baby.
  • In-Person Organization* (4-12 hours): Nina will organize your baby’s clothes, changing table, supplies, and gear, ensuring every item is thoughtfully placed for maximum efficiency and ease. She’ll also set up your nursing station and stock your nursery with essential supplies so that everything you need is always within arm’s reach.
  • Virtual Organization Assistance: If in-person isn’t an option, Nina will guide you through the process remotely, providing expert advice and tailored recommendations.
  • Supplies & Recommendations: Nina will create a checklist of any additional items needed to complete your nursery and make sure you’re nursery is fully stocked and prepared.

Why Clients Love This Service

  • Everything in the nursery is thoughtfully arranged and beautifully organized.

  • Supplies are easy to find and placed in convenient locations.

  • All the hard work is done for you, leaving you free to focus on welcoming your precious baby into a space that’s ready for them.

Take the stress out of nursery setup with Nina’s Nursery Organization service—so all you need to focus on is snuggling your little one!
