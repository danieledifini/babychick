Baby Planning Services

The term “baby planner” is still considered a new term for most people. The metaphor that I like to describe the importance of a baby planner best is that it’s similar to many of the essential experts one hires in their life for guidance and support.

For example:

  • You might hire a wedding planner to help plan your wedding

  • You may work with a financial planner to plan for your retirement and future.

  • You may hire a personal trainer to help you get healthier and into shape.

So, the real question is:

Why wouldn't you hire a baby planner to help prepare you for THE most important event in your life? Becoming a parent!

A baby planner helps busy families prepare for one of life's most important journeys.

We serve as part expert, part best friend.

We can help guide you through:

  • a never-ending list of baby products
  • the highs and lows of pregnancy
  • picking the right pediatrician & childcare option
  • preparing you for childbirth
  • navigating the fourth trimester

And be a sounding board for everything in between & more!

your non-biased bestie and birth & baby expert ALL IN ONE!
